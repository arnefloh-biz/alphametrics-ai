The AI Residency

An AI expert joins your company. Week 1: workflow audit — we shadow every department and map where AI creates the most value. Weeks 2–3: we build real AI solutions with your team. Week 4: handoff — documentation, SOPs, and a trained internal AI champion.

We stay until the work is done.
Teams of 5–15
3 weeks, hybrid

The AI Sprint

A cohort-based program for teams of 5–15. Two live sessions per week plus daily micro-challenges in Slack or Teams. Your team works on their own real workflows — not hypothetical exercises. Week 3 ends with a demo to leadership.

Output: working AI tools. Not a certificate.
Async

The AI Whisper Program

Modelled on Google's Whisper Courses (22–40% measured behavior change). One actionable AI nudge per week, delivered in Slack or Teams. Designed to prevent the post-training decay curve.

Keeps momentum going after the formal engagement ends.

Since February 2025, every company whose employees use AI tools must ensure "a sufficient level of AI literacy." No size exemption. No sector exemption. A 15-person agency using ChatGPT has the same obligation as a 2,000-person manufacturer.
